Rock > Paper > Scissors > Lizard > Spock

A drunken-reflexes, head-to-head iPad game by @helloluis

How to play

Firstly, you must have an iPad (or other horizontally-oriented tablet device) in order to play this.

Second, you must memorize the following:

Scissors cuts Paper covers Rock crushes Lizard poisons Spock smashes Scissors decapitates Lizard eats Paper disproves Spock vaporizes Rock crushes Scissors.

Finally, this game isn't played the way traditional Rock-Paper-Scissors-Lizard-Spock is. In RPSLS for iPad, only one player acts at a time. If the blue player plays Rock, the red player must choose either Paper or Spock, to which the blue player must respond with one of the other options. Play goes back-and-forth for as long as each chosen item is stronger than the last. You score a point if your opponent can't respond in time (they have 1 second), or if they respond with a weaker item.

And now you're ready to play!
